Food

Restaurants

  • Nikkori

    • At Nikkori restaurant the dishes are mostly Asian dishes Including: Sushi Rolls, Chinese Rise, Teppanyaki, etc. It's a nice place to go out for dinner though you might want to make a reservation before hand, otherwise you might end up waiting for a while before you get a tabel.

  • Tacos Uff

    • At Tacos Uff the main dishes there are tacos of many different kinds. They have different meats and different kinds of tortillas. They also offer you the choice to by "Tacos Light" which is practicallly the meat of your choice but replacing the tortilla with a lettuce leaf reducing the amount of calories a person consumes and still making the Tacos quite enjoyable.

  • El Gran Pastor

    • El Gran Pastor is a buffet from 7:00AM-12:00PM. Here the food is probably the best in the whole city and it is a place yu wouldn't want to miss out. The staff here is always checking out and makes sure you are not missing something. This place is a place you will most defenitkly want to go for breakfast.

  • La Purisisma

    • At La Purisima the two most common things that they sell is either Elotes or Trolles it is a great place to go for a quick snack or just to hang out for a while. There are several facilities located around Monterrey in which they all provide the same services and they are all equally good quality.

Culture

In Monterrey people like to party a lot. There are night clubs in a place known as "El Centrito" and everyday you will most likely find poeple partying in there, unless is a special holiday or something like it. And the same way people like to party people also here are very hard workers. People from monterrey or Regios as they are known, have always been hard workers and this is noticable by the amount of buisnesses that have come out of here, businesses such as Cemex, Gamnesa, Axtel, FEMSA, and much more other companies have bloomed from this amazing city thanks to the hard work of the people. As a matter of fct there once was a fabric here in monterrey, it was called fundidora. It gave jobs to practically all of the city and was the symbol of hard work and power of this city. Actually in the city's coat of arms at the bottom right part of it there is a symbol of a fabric. This symbol represents fundidora which was one of the greates fabrics of all México. Unfortunately Monterrey like most cities around the globe doesn't have good drivers, people here are very rough and unthoughtful about the way they drive. It's recommended for you to just call an uber to let someone who is already used to this kind of driving do the driving for you. Also, don't use bicycles for as previously stated people here are not the best drivers. Still, once you make friends on this city I can assure you that those friends will last quite the while. People here do care a lot about friendships and will most likely support you with anything you might need, just don't mess with one of us because you are most likely getting into a conflict with more than one person. Here we all look out for our friends even though we tend to show we don't care much, when one of us is in trouble we stick together and help them get out of the issue. Or we just wish them good luck and leave them stranded to their own luck. That is practically the culture here, we are sort of a family with our friends and yet we still find a way to get along with most people.
